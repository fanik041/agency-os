Applying Meta's design DNA: soft backgrounds, card elevation, blue primary, clean borders

Key Design Traits from Meta/Instagram

Background
Soft gray (#f0f2f5) not pure white. Cards are white on gray. Creates depth without shadows.
Primary Blue
Facebook blue (#1877f2) for CTAs, links, active states. Not overused — accent only.
Borders & Cards
1px solid #dadde1 borders. Cards have border-radius: 8px. Minimal shadow (0 1px 2px rgba). Clean separation.
Typography
System font stack (-apple-system, Segoe UI). Bold headings in #1c1e21. Secondary text in #65676b. Clean hierarchy.

Square badges → pill shape with tinted backgrounds.

Primary: solid #1877f2. Secondary: light blue bg.

Rounded pill search input on gray bg, like IG.

White sidebar, blue active indicator, cleaner icons.
